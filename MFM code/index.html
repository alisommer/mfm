Who We Are

We are a diverse collection of more than 40 vendors gathering every week. Our mission is to bring the freshest produce, meats, eggs, breads, and more to the Morgantown community.

Our products are grown or made by our vendors within 50 miles, so you know that your purchases are supporting small family farms.
